# Loom.com — Design Research

## Color Usage
- Background: `#FFFFFF` — clean white (light theme)
- Primary accent: `#1868DB` — vivid blue, used boldly
- Secondary bg: `#E9F2FE` — very light blue tint
- Text: `#101214` — near-black

## Typography
- Display: **Charlie Display** (custom typeface — rounded, personality-driven)
- Body: **Charlie Text** (matching rounded text variant)
- H1: 70px — largest on page, commanding hero presence
- H2: 48px
- Body: 16px
- Key insight: custom fonts create instant brand distinction; no generic choices

## Layout Patterns
- Pill buttons (`border-radius: 9999px`) — friendly, approachable
- `12px` card border radius
- Full-width hero with centered text
- Product screenshots dominate second half of hero
- Clear visual hierarchy: one CTA wins

## Animation Style
- Smooth, friendly transitions (~300ms ease)
- Product demos autoplay in viewport
- Hover states on cards: subtle lift + shadow

## Hover Effects
- Cards: translateY(-4px) + shadow deepens
- Buttons: color shifts, slight scale
- Interactive elements give clear feedback

## Scroll Interactions
- Sections fade/slide in as user scrolls
- Product features reveal in sequence
- Sticky nav becomes opaque on scroll

## Design Philosophy
> Personality through type. The font IS the brand identity.

---
*Key borrowing opportunity: Custom/characterful display font, bold hero sizing, pill button energy, personality-first design*
