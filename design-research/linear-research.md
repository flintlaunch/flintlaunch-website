# Linear.app — Design Research

## Color Usage
- Background: `#08090A` — near-true OLED black, deeper than typical dark themes
- Text primary: `#D0D6E0` — cool off-white, not pure white
- Accent: `#5E6AD2` — muted purple-blue
- Button primary: `#E6E6E6` bg with `#08090A` text (inverted)
- Button secondary: `#28282C` bg with `#3E3E44` border

## Typography
- Display: SF Pro Display / Inter (system-native feel)
- Body: Inter at 15px
- H1: 64px — commanding but not excessive
- H2: 48px
- Strategy: ultra-tight tracking, almost no decoration

## Layout Patterns
- Near-zero visual noise — everything intentional
- Generous negative space
- Content centered with tight max-width columns
- No gradients on text (kept monochrome)
- Razor-sharp 2px border radius on inputs/buttons — geometric precision
- 4px border radius on primary buttons

## Animation Style
- Barely any — micro-interactions only
- No scroll animations visible on homepage
- Product screenshots slide in with subtle perspective
- Transitions are fast (<200ms)

## Hover Effects
- Minimal — color shift only, no transforms
- Very restrained lift (0-1px max)

## Scroll Interactions
- Sticky nav that barely changes on scroll
- Product imagery parallaxes slightly
- No dramatic reveal animations

## Design Philosophy
> Precision over personality. Every pixel either carries information or is absent.

---
*Key borrowing opportunity: OLED black depth, geometric precision, high-contrast text hierarchy*
