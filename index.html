WARN Content Engine

Layoffs are public record. Every day, companies file WARN Act notices with state governments — and almost no one is watching. This pipeline scrapes 5 states daily, runs each filing through AI to generate video scripts, captions, and posts, then queues everything for one-click publishing to Instagram, TikTok, and X.
